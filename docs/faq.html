Questions

We’re not saying everything yet — here’s the short version.

  • What is SndBnch?

    A place to finish your music. You upload a track, we master it, give it space, and render it into a square or vertical video (plus a cover) you can post anywhere. Think of it as the bench where a rough track becomes something you’re proud to share.

  • Is it live yet?

    Not yet — the engine that does the finishing already runs; we’re building the self-serve front door. Join the waitlist and we’ll tap you the moment you can upload.

  • Do I need to know how to mix or master?

    No. There’s no DAW, no timeline, no plugins. You pick a mastering template (Streaming, Loud, Warm, Broadcast, Vinyl) and a look, and the bench does the work.

  • What’s the “make as many as you want” thing?

    Most tools ration renders because they pay a cloud provider for every one. We run on our own hardware, so one more render costs us almost nothing — which means we don’t need to meter you. Audition a bunch, keep the one you love.

  • Who keeps the rights to my music?

    You do. SndBnch is a tool, not a publisher — you upload music you hold the rights to, we finish and render it, and hand it straight back. Uploads and renders live in a short retention window, then they’re deleted.

  • What will it cost?

    There’ll be a generous free tier and pay-per-render for people who just want to make a lot — payable by card, or in sats over Lightning. Full pricing lands at launch.

  • How do I get in?

    Join the waitlist. One email when the bench opens — no feed, no spam.
