Ch.1 — Runner
The loop · play → keep → render → build

Don't make beats. Play them into being.

01 / PLAY

Play the games

Pick a box from the arcade and play. The world scrolls at the tempo, every pitch is in key — there are no wrong notes and there is no fail state. You just perform.

tap · trigger · slide · draw
02 / PICK

Keep good bars

A run throws off more groove than you need. Scrub back, grab the 1 or 2 bars that felt right, and drop them into a channel. Choosing is the craft.

overgenerate → cull
03 / RENDER

Render a song

Stack seven channels on the deck, add a couple of vibe tags, and hit produce. AceStep turns your loops into a full track — vocals and all.

deck → ace-step → song
04 / POST

Build music vids

The machine renders a square visualization that moves to your song — a video ready to post straight to TikTok, YouTube or X. The season's best get cut to the tape.

song → square viz → post
