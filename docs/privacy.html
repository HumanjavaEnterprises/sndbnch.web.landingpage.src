Privacy Policy

Last updated 2026-07-07 · Pre-launch draft

Short version: the only personal information we collect right now is the email address you give us for the waitlist. We use it to tell you when SndBnch launches, and nothing else. We never sell it.

What we collect

  • Your email address — only if you submit it to join the waitlist.
  • Optional context — if you tell us what you make (e.g. “beats, mixes”), we store it to understand who’s interested. Optional, and free-text.

How we use it

To send you a one-time confirmation link (double opt-in) and, once you confirm, occasional updates about SndBnch’s launch — for up to six months, or until you unsubscribe. That’s it. No feed, no ad targeting, no selling your data.

Who handles the email

Waitlist email is delivered through SendRelay, our own email platform (also operated by Humanjava Enterprises Inc.). Your address is stored to manage your subscription and consent, and isn’t shared with third parties for marketing.

Your choices

  • Unsubscribe anytime from a link in any email.
  • Deletion — email privacy@sndbn.ch and we’ll remove your address from the list.

Your music (when uploads open)

SndBnch is not open for uploads yet. When it is: files you upload are processed to finish and render them, the result is returned to you, and both the upload and the render are deleted after a short retention window. We don’t keep a library of your music or use it to train anything. This policy will be expanded with the full detail before that launches.
