The waitlist

If you submit your email, you’re asking us to notify you about SndBnch’s launch. We’ll email a link to confirm your address (double opt-in), and you can unsubscribe from any message. See our Privacy Policy for how we handle your email.

SndBnch is a tool, not a publisher. When uploads open, you’ll be asked to confirm you hold the rights to any music you upload. You keep all rights to your work. We don’t claim ownership of, publish, or redistribute your music.
