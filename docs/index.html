Why SndBnch can do what the others can’t

Audiogram and waveform-video tools already exist. The difference isn’t the feature—it’s that we own the ground floor.

We own the machine

Everyone else renders on metered cloud GPUs, so they’re forced to ration. Ours are sunk and sovereign—the cost of one more render is close to nothing. That’s why “make as many as you want” is a promise we can actually keep.

A look people recognize

Not the generic bouncing waveform. A layer compositor—a piano-roll spectrogram falling from a centre line, EQ spiking up, a stopwatch perimeter, a palette that shifts on the song’s key changes.

Real finishing, not a filter

Actual mastering templates plus stereo/binaural spatial—the kind of finish musicians already pay for. Kept mono-compatible on purpose, so it still holds up when a phone collapses it to mono.

Pay in cents, not subscriptions

Because a render costs us almost nothing, we can charge per video—in card, or in sats over Lightning—instead of a monthly plan you forget to cancel. A generous free tier stays free.
